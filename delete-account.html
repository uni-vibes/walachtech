Account Deletion

Last updated: January 13, 2026
Support
wlachtechsolutions@gmail.com

You can delete your Uni Vibes account using either of the options below.

Option A — Delete inside the App (recommended)

  1. Open the Uni Vibes app
  2. Go to Profile → Delete Account
  3. Confirm deletion

Option B — Request deletion by email (if you can’t sign in)

Send an email request from the address associated with your account. This helps us verify ownership.

Email template

To: walachtechsolutions@gmail.com
Subject: Account Deletion Request – Uni Vibes

Include:

  • The email/phone number used on your Uni Vibes account
  • Your display name (and campus/university if applicable)
  • Any additional details to help identify the account
  • A clear statement: “I request deletion of my Uni Vibes account and associated personal data.”
Open email request

What gets deleted

When your deletion request is processed, we aim to delete your account profile and associated personal data. Some information may be retained for a limited period for legal, safety, fraud-prevention, dispute resolution, or technical reasons (for example, backups).
